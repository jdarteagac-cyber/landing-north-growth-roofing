1. Introduction

At North Growth Consulting Group, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our services, website, and digital platforms.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us and information that is generated through your use of our services:

2.1 Information You Provide Directly:

  • Contact Data: First name, last name, email address, phone number, mailing address
  • Business Information: Company name, industry, size, geographic location
  • Payment Information: Credit card data (processed through secure payment processors)
  • Communications: Content of emails, messages, and conversations during consulting
  • Documents: Files, reports, and documents shared for analysis

2.2 Information Collected Automatically:

  • Navigation Data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent on site
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies: To improve your experience on our website
  • Device Data: Operating system, device type, unique identifiers

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal data for:

  • Providing, maintaining, and improving our consulting services
  • Processing payments and transactions
  • Communicating with you about services, updates, and changes
  • Responding to your inquiries and support requests
  • Sending marketing information and promotions (only with your consent)
  • Analyzing usage trends to improve our services
  • Complying with legal and regulatory obligations
  • Preventing fraud and unlawful activities
Legal Basis: We process your information based on: consent, contract fulfillment, legal compliance, and our legitimate interests.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except in the following cases:

4.1 Service Providers:

We share information with third-party providers who help us operate:

  • Payment processors (Stripe, PayPal, etc.)
  • Cloud storage providers (AWS, Google Cloud)
  • Email and communication providers
  • Analytics and CRM tools

4.2 Legal Compliance:

We will disclose information when required by law, court order, or government authority request.

4.3 Business Transfers:

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you of any change in ownership or control of your information.

5. Data Security

We implement technical, administrative, and physical measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
  • Data encryption at rest
  • Firewalls and intrusion detection systems
  • Restricted access based on need-to-know basis
  • Regular security audits
  • Confidentiality agreements with all employees
Important Note: While we implement robust security measures, we cannot guarantee absolute data security. Internet transmission always carries certain risks.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide the contracted services
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations
  • Resolve disputes and enforce our legal rights

Generally, we retain active client data during the business relationship and up to 7 years after termination for tax and legal purposes. You can request deletion of your data at any time, subject to legal limitations.

7. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

Right Description
Access Obtain a copy of your personal data
Rectification Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
Deletion Request deletion of your data ("Right to be Forgotten")
Restriction Limit how we use your data
Portability Receive your data in a structured, transferable format
Objection Object to certain types of processing
Non-Discrimination Not be discriminated against for exercising your rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information in the Contact section.

8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analyze site usage and user behavior
  • Improve site functionality and content
  • Personalize your experience

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our site.

9. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies before providing personal information.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices, technology, and applicable laws. We will notify you of significant changes by email or by posting the revised policy on our website. Your continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18 years of age. We do not intentionally collect personal information from minors. If we discover that we have collected data from a minor without verifiable parental consent, we will delete it immediately.

12. Jurisdiction-Specific Privacy

12.1 For Users in Colombia (LPDP):

Under Colombia's Personal Data Protection Law, you have additional rights including access, rectification, and deletion of data. We may process data to comply with Colombian legal obligations.

12.2 For Users in the United States:

We comply with applicable state privacy laws including CCPA (California), HIPAA (when applicable), and other regulations. For California residents, you have specific CCPA rights.

12.3 For Users in Europe (GDPR):

Although we do not specifically direct our services to Europe, if we process data of European residents, we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including Privacy Impact Assessments and Data Transfer Agreements.
